Program Closings

Two flagship national events that close the program — one for Emergency Care, one for Disaster / MCM. Each consolidates the six months of work into a single day of recognition, showcase, and forward-look with broader stakeholders.

Rules of this layer

  • Two events — one for each program track (EC and Disaster/MCM)
  • 1-day national — Thursday format, single-day flagship
  • EC Closing — Thu 10-Dec-2026 (Week 26)
  • D/MCM Closing — Thu 17-Dec-2026 (Week 27)
  • Trainer / facilitator team — 5 Master + 5 Local (flagship scale)
  • Attendees — 50 per event (broader stakeholder audience: senior officials, trainers, champions)
