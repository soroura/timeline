Monthly Process / Disaster TOT

A single 3-day TOT each month, alternating between the EC Process track (P-TOT) and the Disaster / MCM track (D-TOT). Each one renews specialist trainer capacity in its domain.

Rules of this layer

  • Cadence — one TOT per month, alternating: EC P-TOT in odd months, Disaster/MCM TOT in even months
  • Duration — 3 days each (Tue–Thu of Week 3, separate trainer team)
  • Trainers — 3 Master + 2 Local (specialist team, distinct from BEC trainer pool)
  • Trainees — 20 per TOT (drawn nationally from the 6 entities)
  • Parallel to BEC base — runs alongside Layer 1, no conflict because trainer teams are separate
  • Output over 6 months — 3 EC P-TOT + 3 D-TOT = 6 TOTs · 18 training days · 120 trainees
