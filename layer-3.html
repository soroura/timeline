Hospital Microlearning

Eight-week microlearning at four hospitals in parallel — two on the Disaster / MCM track and two on the Emergency Care track. Each cohort culminates in a hospital-led drill or a Quality Improvement project showcase, then shifts to four new hospitals.

Rules of this layer

  • 4 hospitals per 8-week cohort — 2 on Disaster/MCM, 2 on Emergency Care
  • Weekly rhythm per hospital — 1 half-day microlearning session (Thu, in parallel across hospitals)
  • Week 8 culmination — Disaster/MCM hospitals run an implementation + drill; EC hospitals showcase a quality-improvement project (each 1 full day)
  • Trainer model — 1 Master Trainer per hospital per session + group of in-hospital champions (champions = hospital staff, not part of trainer pool)
  • Cohort rotation — after each 8-week cohort, shift to 4 brand-new hospitals
  • 6-month output — 3 cohorts · 12 unique hospitals · 80 microlearning sessions · 12 culmination events · (Phase-in: C1 starts W3; C3 is 7 wks)
