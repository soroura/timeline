Basic Emergency Care — Base Stream

Foundation layer running parallel to all other program streams. Two BEC events per week (one full, one hybrid) rotate across six entities, with a national TOT seeded at the start of each month.

Rules of the stream

  • Foundation, not competing — runs alongside every other stream, every week
  • Weekly rhythm — 1 BEC4 (4-day full) + 1 BEC2 (2-day hybrid) per week, parallel cohorts, two trainer teams
  • Monthly output — 4 BEC4 + 4 BEC2 = 8 BEC events per month
  • Monthly TOT — 2-day national TOT on Wed–Thu of Week 1 of each month (M1 skipped as phase-in), separate trainer team
  • Entity rotation — EHA · SMC · PHC · EAO · Zamala · MOH (round-robin, each entity hosts 1 BEC every 3 weeks)
  • Trainer mix — 3 Master + 2 Local per event (Phase 1 default; ramps later when other streams layer in)
